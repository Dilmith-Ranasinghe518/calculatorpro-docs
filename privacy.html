Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CalculatorPro handles information when you use our app.

1. Personal Data Collection

CalculatorPro does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

No login, registration, email, or personal identification details are required to use our app.

2. Information You Enter in the App

All values entered—such as salary, EPF percentages, gratuity years, unit conversion values—are processed locally on your device. None of these values are sent to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

CalculatorPro uses publicly available APIs to fetch:

  • Country names and flags (from restcountries.com)
  • EPF/ETF data hosted on GitHub

These services may collect non-personal diagnostic data such as request logs. CalculatorPro does not control or store this information.

4. Advertising and Analytics

The free version of CalculatorPro may include third-party advertisements (AdMob or similar). These services may collect device identifiers or ad interaction data to improve ad relevance.

We do not store or access this information.

5. Security

Since CalculatorPro does not process or store personal data, risk exposure is minimal. All calculations occur on your device without external transmission.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided information, contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
