Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CalculatorPro, published by Calcify Labs, handles information when you use our application.

1. Personal Data Collection

CalculatorPro does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

No login, registration, email address, or personally identifiable information is required to use this app.

2. Information You Enter in the App

All values entered into the app (such as salary figures, EPF/ETF percentages, gratuity years, unit conversion values, or calculator inputs) are processed locally on your device.

None of this data is transmitted, stored, or shared with external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

CalculatorPro may use publicly available APIs to retrieve:

  • Country names and flags (via restcountries.com)
  • EPF / ETF reference data hosted on GitHub

These third-party services may collect limited non-personal information such as standard request logs. Calcify Labs does not control, store, or access this data.

4. Advertising and Analytics

The free version of CalculatorPro may display third-party advertisements (such as Google AdMob).

These ad providers may collect device identifiers or ad interaction data to improve ad performance and relevance, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Calcify Labs does not collect, store, or have access to this information.

5. Security

Since CalculatorPro does not collect or store personal data, security risks are minimal. All calculations are performed locally on the user’s device without external transmission.

6. Children’s Privacy

CalculatorPro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
